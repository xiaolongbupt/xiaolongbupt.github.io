Biography

Xiaolong Zheng is currently a post-doctoral researcher at School of Software, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Yunhao Liu. He received his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Yunhao Liu and Prof. Lionel M. Ni. He is a member of the Systems and Ubiquitous Networking (SUN) group in the Institute of Trustworthy Networks and Systems, working with Dr. Yuan He.

Research Interests

  • Wireless networking: Cross-technology communication; Coexistence; Low-power wireless networking
  • Cyber-physical systems: Smart sensing systems; Human-centric computing
