Biography

Xiaolong Zheng is currently a professor in School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He is also an affiliated member of State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology. Before joining BUPT, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University. He received his Ph.D. degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2015 and his B.E. degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2011.

Xiaolong Zheng leads the Wireless Intelligent Networking System (WINS) Group, collaborating with a talented team of students on networking and system research. The networking topics includes satellite LPWAN, cross-technology communication, and cross-medium communication; the system topics encompass OS for AI (mobile AI and embedded AI) and integrated sensing and communication.
