Biography

Xiaolong Zheng is currently an Associate Professor and Ph.D. supervisor in School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He is also an affiliated member of Beijing Key Lab of Intelligent Telecommunication Software and Multimedia. During 2015.09-2018.08, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University. He received his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2015, and his B.E. degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2011.

Research Interests

  • Wireless networking: Cross-technology communication; Wireless coexistence; Heterogeneous networking; LPWAN; Backscatter
  • Intelligent computing: Intelligent sensing; Ubiquitous computing; Embedded systems
