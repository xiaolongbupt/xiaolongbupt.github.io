Biography

Xiaolong Zheng is currently an associate researcher and Ph.D. supervisor in School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. Before that, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, during 2015.09-2018.08. He received his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2015, and his B.E. degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2011.

Research Interests

  • Wireless networking: Cross-technology communication; Wireless coexistence; Heterogeneous networking; Backscatter communication
  • Ubiquitous computing: Ubiquitous sensing; Embedded systems; Wearable sensing
